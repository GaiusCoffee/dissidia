# Welcome to Dissidia Tabletop RPG The Dissidia Tabletop RPG is a Pen and Paper roleplaying game that is inspired by [Square Enix's Final Fantasy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Final_Fantasy_%28video_game%29) series as well as [Returner Games' FFRPG](http://ffrpg.net/ffrpg). Dissidia is designed from the groud up to be minimal and modular, as well as a [Minmaxer's Delight](http://tvtropes.org/pmwiki/pmwiki.php/Main/MinmaxersDelight). This rulebook is divided into crystal shards, with chapters that is usually summarized in one or two paragraphs, with a more detailed explanation afterwards.
